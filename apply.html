INTERNSHIPS

TEAI offers a number of opportunities for those who believe that their small actions can result in a large difference. The opportunities offered by us are as follows:

  • Summer Internship Program (5th June – 5th August).
  • Winter Internship Program (20th December -20th February).

Summer/Winter Internship Program (5th June/20thDecember – 5th August/20th February)*:

Summer Internship Program comprises of events such as :

  • Surveys
  • Leadership & Management Activities
  • Participation in Events of Russian Centre and American Centre
  • Volunteering
  • Earth and Space Awareness Program

*Students applying for winter internship can apply for either 1 or 2 month program as per their suitability.

Internship Form

Research Internship Program (3 months – 6 months):

Interns opting for Research internship program are bound for at least 3, although the internship can be extended if the intern wishes to and the organization feels the need to.

Responsibilities and Work Culture: The interns opting this particular internship will be required to fulfill the following responsibilities:

  • Researching on a particular topic in the Field of Earth and Space
  • Volunteering in School Programs
  • Report in office for at least 1 day in a week
  • Making a proper report
