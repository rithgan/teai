About Us

TOE's Earth and Astronomy Initiative

TEAI is the Science and Technology Initiative of Tears of the EARTH Organization with the aim of protecting the Earth’s Environment and reaches for the horizons with spreading the wings of knowledge and making aware every individual out there about Environment and Space.

The motto behind this Initiative of the TOE is introducing the youth, especially the students to the emerging and futuristic field, which is a combination of Environment and Space. The spectrum of knowledge is very vast from Monitoring Earth to know the changes, knowing Satellite Communication with the basic knowledge of technologies near-Earth space.

With the support of the society, utilization of time and help of so many other organizations with the same concern and motive as ours, we are doing different things every day and keep exploring what more better we can do.

Our Major Programs are:

  • Earth and Space Awareness programs for Schools and Colleges
  • Components’ Workshop for School Students
  • Innovation centre projects with Different Colleges
  • Research Internship
  • Volunteering and Participation Winter and Summer Internships for College Students
